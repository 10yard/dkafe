NES Console - Zelda Kong III (Moblin Edition)

This hack changes virtually all graphics and palettes
in Donkey Kong to resemble The Legend Of Zelda. 
Climb up from the bottom of the dungeon to stop the
big bad and save your friend!

The good guys are the bad guys! Play as a Moblin, 
humiliate Link, and rescue your Goriya friend!
Smash the stupid heroes with a huge hunk of meat!
Collect Rupees and Bombs for bonus points.
Escape the clutches of Merchants, Old Women, 
Whirlwinds, Zelda herself, and Old Men hiding in the 
bushes.

Hack Developed by Zero Meaning
