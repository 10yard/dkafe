Atari 800XL - Donkey Kong

Donkey Kong for the Atari 8 bit computers was the best
Donkey Kong that was made except for the arcade 
version.
The Atari 800 Donkey Kong has all four screens. Unlike 
the Colecovision, NES and Atari 7800 versions. It even 
includes the How high can you get? intermissions.

Developed By Nintendo/Landon Dyer
