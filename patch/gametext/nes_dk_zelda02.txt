NES Console - Zelda Kong II (Zelda Edition)

This hack changes virtually all graphics and palettes
in Donkey Kong to resemble The Legend Of Zelda. 
Climb up from the bottom of the dungeon to stop the
big bad and save your friend!

Play as Zelda, destroy an injured Ganon, and save Link!

As a user of the arcane arts, the princess prefers a 
Magic Wand to defeat enemies.
Collect Triforce Pieces and Light Arrows for bonus 
points.
Confront Gibdos, Bubbles, Wizzrobes, Ganon Dust, Pols
Voice, Wallmasters... and the Old Woman.

Hack Developed by Zero Meaning
