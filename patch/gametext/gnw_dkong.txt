Game & Watch - Donkey Kong DK-52

Mario starts at the bottom left of the bottom screen.
Move right and dodge Donkey Kong's barrels by pressing
the jump button. Climb the ladder to the second girder
and move back to the left. When he climbs the next 
ladder he will appear on the top screen.
Once on the top screen, the player can press Left to 
activate the crane, then move to the right dodging 
more barrels. When the crane swings to the left, Mario
can Jump to grab it and cut wires holding DK's girder. 

Developed By Nintendo
