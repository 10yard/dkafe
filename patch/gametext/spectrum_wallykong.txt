ZX Spectrum - Wally Kong

Battle your way to the top of the screen avoiding the
numerous cannonballs rolling towards you. Use all your
skill and judgement to avoid Wally Kong's efforts to 
stop you, while you attempt to negotiate all four 
levels of this excellent high resolution graphics 
arcade style game, which includes ladders, lifts, 
moving platforms and numerous other hazards.

Developed By Walltone Software
