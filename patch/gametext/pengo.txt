Pengo

Pengo is a 2D action arcade game that takes place in a
maze-like environment filled with ice blocks and 
enemies.

The gameplay involves moving Pengo around the maze 
using the joystick and pushing the ice blocks to 
defeat the enemies.

The main objective of the game is to collect all the 
diamonds scattered throughout the maze while avoiding 
and defeating the enemies.

Developed by Sega
