NES Console - Ice Cream!

Is this a dream? No, it is not, sadly. Go forth unto
the evil Ice Cream Factory and save your friend Irene.
Stopping the evil Giant Ice Cream Cone from 
terrorizing the world is your number one duty.

The factory spans three levels, each with their own 
tricks and traps.

Others have come before you, but have failed. 
Irene is waiting for you to save her. Someone must 
answer the call; Terror must not reign over the land.
