NES Console - Zelda Kong (Link Edition)

This hack changes virtually all graphics and palettes
in Donkey Kong to resemble The Legend Of Zelda. 
Climb up from the bottom of the dungeon to stop the
big bad and save your friend!

Play as Link, defeat Ganon, and free Zelda!
Grab a trusty Sword power-up to vanquish your foes!
Collect Keys and Rafts for bonus points.
Face off against Stalfos, Fire, Ghinis, Traps, 
Tektites, Ropes... and the Old Man.

Hack Developed by Zero Meaning
