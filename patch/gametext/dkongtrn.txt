Donkey Kong Trainer

This trainer plays just like normal Donkey Kong, but 
adds extra information to the game's display and
includes features and functions to help with practice
and training.

- Both your total score and board score are shown
- Points that could be earned leaching Kong is shown
- The game speed (selectable between 20% and 150%)
- Points earned from jumps and hammer smashes is shown
- Internal difficulty is shown
- Remaining hammer time is shown
- Information about bouncing springs is shown

and much more...
