NES Console - Bowsette Junior

Donkey Kong and Donkey Kong Jr characters are replaced
with Bowsette and Bowsette Junior respectively.
Jumpman is replaced by Toadette.

Items are changed to MechaKoopas, Bomb-ombs and Koopa 
shells.
All enemies are based on Captain Toad Treasure Tracker.
