NES Console - Retro vs Golmonger DK Edition

After being completely fed up of constantly getting 
pummeled by Googie, Golmonger decides to set his 
sights on Retro. Knowing that he’s friends with

then decides to kidnap one of Retro’s friends Dejiko

amazing hero Retro to give Golmonger the boot by
