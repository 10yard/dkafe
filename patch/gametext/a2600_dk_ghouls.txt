Atari 2600 Console - Ghouls'n'Ghosts Hack

There are 4 levels and 2 Bosses to be defeated to 
rescue your loved one, Princess Prim Prim.
King Sardius and Loki joined forces to defeat the 
fearless knight, King Arthur.
