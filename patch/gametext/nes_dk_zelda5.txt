NES Console - Zelda Kong V (Armos Edition)

This hack changes virtually all graphics and palettes
in Donkey Kong to resemble The Legend Of Zelda. 
Climb up from the bottom of the dungeon to stop the
big bad and save your friend!

Your Octorok pal's going to be Aquamentus' next snack!
Play as an ancient Armos guardian and get your friend 
back to safety.
Crush all who oppose you with that Bracelet you've 
been sitting on for the past 100 years.
Collect Rings and Whistles for bonus points.
Face the dread dragon's army of Dodongos, Manhandla 
Maws, Lynels, and Darknuts.
