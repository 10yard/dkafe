Crazy Kong Bootleg on Moon Cresta Hardware

This bootleg of Crazy Kong was made to be played on
Moon Cresta hardware.

This clone retains all of the gameplay elements of 
Donkey Kong, but has the graphics redrawn and 
re-colorised. 

Push "Jump" button to start after inserting coins 
instead of the "Player 1" button.

A Crazy Kong Bootleg (originally by Falcon)
