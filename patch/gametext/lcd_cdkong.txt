LCD - Coleco Tabletop Donkey Kong

Plays and sounds like the Donkey Kong arcade game!
Well,  that's what it says in the instruction manual,
along with the following.

"You'll find that this tabletop game is full of special
features to make Donkey Kong exciting every time you
play.  Experiment with different techniques - and 
enjoy the game!"

You get to play the barrels stage and the rivets 
stages only.

Developed By Coleco
