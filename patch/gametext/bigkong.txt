Big Kong

That crazy monkey has carried off your girlfriend. 
You must rescue her at the top of the screen by 
traversing beams, climbing ladders and riding 
elevators while avoiding barrels, fireballs and pies.

Ladders on the barrel stage are in different positions
and some rise much higher than usual. They can be used 
to ascend two or three beams at a time, which actually 
makes the game more difficult.
