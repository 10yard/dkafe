NES Console - Zelda Kong 6 (Link 2 Edition)

This hack changes virtually all graphics and palettes
in Donkey Kong to resemble The Legend Of Zelda. 
Climb up from the bottom of the dungeon to stop the
big bad and save your friend!

Is Link smaller or are all his enemies huge? 
The Thunderbird has Zelda in its claws, and you must
save her, because she can't save herself for 
some reason.
Demolish everything in your path with the Hammer.
Pick up Dungeon Keys and Treasure Bags for a bonus.
Encounter Strong Enemies, Bubbles, Octoroks, Myu, 
Megmats, and Japanese River Devils.

Hack Developed by Zero Meaning
