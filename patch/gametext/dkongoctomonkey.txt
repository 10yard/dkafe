Octo Monkey

An extremely short game of Donkey Kong.

You play 1/8 of a game with 8 times the points awarded
e.g. you get 800 points for jumping a single barrel!
 
The game starts at level 19-4 (elevator stage) with 1
life and no bonus lives.

Can you get to the Kill Screen with 1 million points?

Hack Developed by Jon Wilson (10yard)
