ZX Spectrum 48K - Wrath of Kong

Beautiful Janes has been abducted by save Kong. Her
rescue requires a perilous venture into Kong's lair
(one of 4 action structures) during which you will 
face tumbling barrels and trap doors.  Skillful use
of the hammer and broken ladders is your protection.

Only great agility and cunning will outwit Kong.
Climb quickly or Jane will perish.
