Donkey Kong Annversary Edition

Celebrating 40 years of the Donkey Kong arcade game. 
The original game was released on July 9th 1981. 

Donkey Kong and Pauline have decided to throw a party
to celebrate the anniversary. They've invited Jumpman 
and it's your job to take him to the party. 

You should collect presents and balloons on your way.
The Boards are all variations of the original.

Have fun ... and celebrate !!!
