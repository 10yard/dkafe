Donkey Kong Lava Pies

Jumpman must keep his cool and move quickly up the 
platforms to avoid rising Lava.  Don't let the lava
rise above Jumpman.

This is a variation of DK LavaPanic were you play
only the pies stage starting from level 5. 

The lava rises much more quickly from level 5.
Try not to panic!
